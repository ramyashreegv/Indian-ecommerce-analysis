




# Indian E-Commerce Sales Performance & Pricing Analysis.

**Project Objective:**

This project analyzes 36 months of e-commerce transaction data to understand transaction performance, regional demand clusters, customer generation buying behaviors, and the business impacts of promotional discount pricing strategy.

**Tools Used:**
* Microsoft Excel: Data profiling, quality assurance, and age segment feature engineering.
* Tableau Public: Interactive data visualization, exploratory analysis, and dashboard architecture.

**Live Interactive Dashboard:**
Link to the interactive Tableau Dashboard: https://public.tableau.com/views/Indian_ecommerce_pricing_revenue_growth_36_months/GenerationBuyingPatterns?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link



**Key Insights and Visualizations:**

**1. Generation Buying Patterns:**
* Chart Type: Stacked Bar Chart.
* Insight: The under 25 age group drives the highest order volume in Fashion and Electronics, whereas the 35 to 54 demographic represents a steady recurring baseline for Grocery Essentials.

**2. Revenue Performance Trend:**
* Chart Type: 36-Month Timeline Line Chart
* Insight: Revenue displays a strong cyclical surge corresponding directly with regional Festival calendar phases, jumping significantly compared to Normal sales periods.

**3. Zonewise Sales:**
* Chart Type: Stacked Bar Chart.
* Insight: The West and South zones dominate platform market share, contributing a major portion of absolute platform revenue, anchored heavily by states like Maharashtra and Karnataka.

**4. Discount vs. Units Sold:**
* Chart Type: Scatter Plot (Unaggregated).
* Insight: While extreme discounts increase units sold for Mass market goods, Premium category products preserve steady transaction rates without needing deep price cuts.

**5. Category vs. Brand Type Analysis**
* Chart Type: Clustered Side-by-Side Bar Chart.
* Insight: Mass products outpace Premium variants in volume across all core categories except for Premium Lifestyle, which maintains an independent high-margin baseline.

**Business Recommendations:**
Based on the completed dashboards, the platform can deploy three primary strategic adjustments:

* Targeted Seasonal Advertising: Heavy-up digital display marketing allocation towards the West and South zones roughly 3 weeks ahead of scheduled Festival event periods.

* Margin Optimization: Dial back discount rates on Premium brand categories, as sales metrics show their customer demographic exhibits lower price sensitivity.

* Age Cohort Customization: Surface Fashion and Electronics categories on the mobile application homepage exclusively when users identified under the under 25 age segment log in.
